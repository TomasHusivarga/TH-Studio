# Blog Writing Style

Use this file before writing or editing blog posts for the portfolio.

## Goal

Blog posts should sound like Tomáš thinking through a real web topic in plain language. The topic can be a problem, plugin decision, WooCommerce setup, WordPress fix, SEO observation, speed issue, pricing/process note, content decision, or something learned during a project. The writing should feel direct, practical, slightly informal, and honest. It should not sound like an agency blog, SEO filler, or polished corporate advice.

## Voice

- Write in Slovak for public blog posts unless the user asks for English.
- Use first person when it makes the article more real: `riešil som`, `pozeral som`, `všimol som si`, `upravil som`.
- Keep the tone conversational but clean. Do not publish raw typos or chat shorthand.
- It is okay to be direct: `toto samo o sebe nič nerieši`, `tu bol problém`, `toto nie je signál záujmu`.
- Prefer practical wording over fancy wording.
- Use normal Slovak sentences. Avoid awkward phrases that sound translated from English.

## What It Should Feel Like

Good:

- `Na prvý pohľad to vyzeralo, že web má problém. Keď som sa pozrel do Search Console, ukázalo sa niečo iné.`
- `Impresia ešte neznamená, že človek hľadal službu. Niekedy Google zobrazí portfólio pri názve klienta, čo má úplne iný zámer.`
- `Toto nie je veľká technická zmena, ale pomáha Googlu lepšie pochopiť, ktoré stránky sú služby a ktoré sú iba ukážky práce.`

Bad:

- `V dnešnej digitálnej dobe je SEO neoddeliteľnou súčasťou úspechu každej firmy.`
- `Naša spoločnosť poskytuje komplexné riešenia na mieru.`
- `Objavte silu optimalizácie a posuňte svoje podnikanie na novú úroveň.`

## Structure

Use simple, readable structure. For problem posts:

1. What happened
2. Why it mattered
3. What was checked
4. What was changed
5. What to watch next

For broader topic posts, use:

1. What the topic is
2. Why people usually get it wrong or underestimate it
3. What I would check or choose
4. Practical examples
5. Short takeaway

Do not over-explain. If a section does not add anything useful, skip it.

## Vocabulary

Prefer:

- `web`, `stránka`, `e-shop`, `WordPress`, `WooCommerce`
- `problém`, `úprava`, `oprava`, `kontrola`, `výsledok`
- `dotaz`, `impresia`, `klik`, `Search Console`, `interný odkaz`
- `zámer človeka`, `služba`, `portfólio`, `projektová stránka`

Avoid or use rarely:

- `digitálna transformácia`
- `komplexné riešenia`
- `prémiový`
- `inovatívny`
- `maximalizovať potenciál`
- `posunúť biznis na ďalší level`

## SEO Rules

- Write for a person first, but include the real words people search for where they naturally fit.
- Do not stuff keywords.
- Include internal links to relevant service pages when useful:
  - `/TH-Studio/sluzby/tvorba-webstranok.html`
  - `/TH-Studio/sluzby/tvorba-eshopov.html`
  - `/TH-Studio/sluzby/uprava-wordpress-webu.html`
- If the article comes from a real project/problem, keep client details public-safe and broad.
- Do not reveal private emails, admin screens, analytics details, payments, disputes, or internal client notes.

## Editing Checklist

Before publishing a blog post:

- Does it sound like a real person explaining a real topic or decision?
- Is there at least one concrete detail from actual work?
- Are the headings natural in Slovak?
- Is there a useful internal link to a service page?
- Did we avoid confidential/private details?
- Did we avoid corporate filler?
