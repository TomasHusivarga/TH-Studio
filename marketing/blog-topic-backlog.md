# Blog Topic Backlog

Use this as a practical SEO/content backlog for TH Studio blog posts. The goal is to attract people who are already trying to set up, fix, or understand a WordPress/WooCommerce website.

## Evidence Used

- Existing blog topics already cover AI workflow, WordPress migration, infected-site cleanup, pricing, business websites, Search Console impressions, and WooCommerce diagnostics.
- Search Console has already shown repair/pricing intent such as `oprava webu wordpress`, `wordpress cena`, and WooCommerce problem wording.
- Google Trends automated access was blocked with HTTP 429, so this list does not claim exact Trends scores.
- Google autocomplete returned useful long-tail confirmations for topics like `wordpress smtp`, `contact form 7`, `wordpress zaloha`, `wordpress virus`, `woocommerce packeta`, and `woocommerce problem`.
- Official documentation supports the recurring setup/problem areas: WooCommerce shipping zones, WooCommerce email troubleshooting, SMTP/email providers, Google Trends, and Keyword Planner.

## Priority 1: Most Likely To Bring Useful Leads

### 1. Prečo WordPress neposiela e-maily a ako to opraviť

- Intent: someone has a broken form, order email, password reset, or admin notification.
- Target phrases: `WordPress neposiela e-maily`, `WordPress SMTP`, `kontaktný formulár neposiela email`, `WP Mail SMTP`.
- Angle: plain explanation that WordPress mail is often unreliable without SMTP; show what to check before panicking.
- Internal link: `/TH-Studio/sluzby/uprava-wordpress-webu.html`.
- Lead value: high. People searching this usually need help now.

### 2. Ako nastaviť kontaktný formulár vo WordPresse, aby reálne fungoval

- Intent: beginner wants a contact form, or form exists but submissions are not arriving.
- Target phrases: `kontaktný formulár WordPress`, `Contact Form 7`, `WordPress formulár`, `formulár neposiela email`.
- Angle: contact form is not just fields on a page; it needs email delivery, spam handling, thank-you message, and testing.
- Internal link: `/TH-Studio/sluzby/tvorba-webstranok.html`.
- Lead value: high for small business websites.

### 3. Ako nastaviť dopravu vo WooCommerce

- Intent: store owner is setting delivery zones, shipping methods, Packeta/Zásielkovňa, local pickup or courier.
- Target phrases: `WooCommerce doprava`, `ako nastaviť dopravu WooCommerce`, `WooCommerce Packeta`, `WooCommerce Zásielkovňa`.
- Angle: explain zones, methods, order of zones, free shipping, local pickup, and why shipping sometimes does not show in checkout.
- Internal link: `/TH-Studio/sluzby/tvorba-eshopov.html`.
- Lead value: high. Shipping setup is a common e-shop blocker.

### 4. Prečo sa vo WooCommerce nezobrazuje doprava v pokladni

- Intent: direct problem search.
- Target phrases: `WooCommerce nezobrazuje dopravu`, `WooCommerce shipping problem`, `doprava sa nezobrazuje v pokladni`, `WooCommerce problém`.
- Angle: diagnostic post: shipping zones, address match, enabled methods, product shipping class, cache/plugin conflict.
- Internal link: `/TH-Studio/sluzby/uprava-wordpress-webu.html`.
- Lead value: very high because it is repair intent, not casual reading.

### 5. Ako nastaviť platby vo WooCommerce bez zbytočného chaosu

- Intent: beginner wants card payment, bank transfer, cash on delivery, Stripe/PayPal/local gateway.
- Target phrases: `WooCommerce platby`, `WooCommerce platobná brána`, `platba kartou WooCommerce`, `dobierka WooCommerce`.
- Angle: what payment methods make sense at the start, what gateway needs, and what to test before launch.
- Internal link: `/TH-Studio/sluzby/tvorba-eshopov.html`.

## Article Template

- Start with the real problem in one plain paragraph.
- Explain why it usually happens.
- Give a short checklist of what to inspect.
- Mention when it is safe to do yourself and when it is better to ask someone.
- Link naturally to the matching service page.
- Keep the ending practical, not motivational.
